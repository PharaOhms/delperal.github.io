    What is a hacker?

    • In general, people have a mistaken concept and totally removed from reality about the meaning of what a hacker is or how he acts, in part society, movies and series have been in charge of denigrating the sector since a person who does not have knowledge on the subject will be influenced and misinformed about the work and principles that hackers have.
    • To begin with, I must clarify that unfortunately not all of us have the intention of taking advantage of our knowledge and skills to do good...
      There are many definitions of what a hacker is but personally I feel identified with the following:

    • A passionate, an enthusiast, an expert in new technologies, a person who tries to break the limits of technology to create something superior, who considers that making information available to everyone constitutes an extraordinary good.
